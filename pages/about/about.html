Our Story

It all began with our founder, Mr. Mayank, who started out with a few houseplants that grew into a full-fledged obsession. What began as a hobby quickly blossomed into a mission to help others discover the same sense of joy and connection that plants bring. Whether you're looking for a statement plant for your living room, a low-maintenance addition to your office, or a vibrant new plant for your kitchen window, E-Plants offers a variety of plants to suit every lifestyle and space.
